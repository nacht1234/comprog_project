GAME MECHANICS:
A. SHAPE ONLY MODE
- A RANDOM TARGET SHAPE WILL BE SHOWN FOR 5 SECONDS
- THE PLAYER'S GOAL IS TO FIND AND CLICK ON THE SHAPE THAT CORRESPONDS TO THE TARGET SHAPE BEFORE THE TIMER RUNS OUT

B. SHAPE WITH COLOR MODE
- A RANDOM TARGET SHAPE WITH  A RANDOM COLOR WILL BE SHOWN FOR 5 SECONDS
- THE PLAYER'S GOAL IS TO FIND AND CLICK ON THE COLORED SHAPE THAT CORRESPONDS TO THE TARGET COLORED SHAPE BEFORE THE TIMER RUNS OUT
